[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Caught in the middle of a midnight gaze
Dancing through the shadows in a candlelit haze
I feel the pull of your gravity now
Losing my breath but I do not care how

[Verse 1]
The fire is burning down to embers and ash
Shadows are playing while the moments pass
You pull me closer with a single look
Reading my mind like an open book

[Pre-Chorus]
The world outside is fading to black
There is no turning, no looking back
Every heartbeat echoes your name
Moths flying blindly into the flame

[Chorus]
Oh, I am caught in your spell tonight
Wrapped in the warmth of the fading light
You are the magic I can't undo
Losing myself in the gravity of you

[Post-Chorus]
Caught in your spell, drifting so deep
A beautiful promise I want to keep

[Verse 2]

[Bridge]
